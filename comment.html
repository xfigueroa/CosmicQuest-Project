FAQS

  • What is Cosmic Quest? +

    • Cosmic Quest is an otherworldly event taking place on the red sands of Mars. It's a unique fusion of science, art, and adventure, where participants can explore the mysteries of the Martian landscape, attend captivating talks by interplanetary experts, and witness breathtaking celestial phenomena.

  • How do I get to Mars for Cosmic Quest?+

    • As for now we have the Cosmic Uber option, where you just have to open your Cosmic Uber app. Request a ride to Mars. Choose from options like “Standard Rocket,” “Hyperloop Wormhole,” or “Flying Saucer Pool Party.” Your driver, Zog the Nebula Navigator, will arrive in approximately 3.5 light-years. Don't forget to tip in stardust!

  • What activities can I expect during Cosmic Quest?+

    • During Cosmic Quest, expect stargazing sessions, Martian art exploration, alien mixology workshops, astro-trivia challenges, and live Q&A with a Martian geologist!

  • Is there a dress code for Cosmic Quest?+

    • Absolutely! We encourage attendees to embrace their inner Martian. Think metallic silver jumpsuits, holographic accessories, and futuristic headgear. Bonus points for antennae or third-eye prosthetics.

  • Will there be Wi-Fi on Mars during Cosmic Quest?+

    • While Mars itself lacks Wi-Fi hotspots (for now), our mission control team has you covered. Connect to our CosmicNet™ network for uninterrupted streaming of talks, workshops, and alien dance-offs.
